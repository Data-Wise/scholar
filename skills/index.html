    Statistical Research Skills¶

    17 A-grade auto-activating skills for statistical research and methods development.

    Skill Categories¶

    • Mathematical (4 skills)
    • Implementation (5 skills)
    • Writing (3 skills)
    • Research (5 skills)

    Mathematical Skills¶

    proof-architect¶

    Rigorous proof construction and validation.

    Expertise: - Theorem-lemma structure - Mathematical completeness - Logical flow - Assumption validation

    Activates on: "proof", "theorem", "lemma", "identifiability"

    mathematical-foundations¶

    Statistical theory foundations.

    Expertise: - Probability theory - Measure-theoretic statistics - Asymptotic theory - Stochastic processes

    Activates on: "theory", "foundations", "mathematical", "rigorous"

    identification-theory¶

    Parameter identifiability analysis.

    Expertise: - Causal identification - Non-parametric identification - Instrumental variables - Sensitivity analysis

    Activates on: "identifiability", "causal", "instrumental variables"

    asymptotic-theory¶

    Large-sample theory.

    Expertise: - Consistency - Asymptotic normality - Weak convergence - Delta method

    Activates on: "asymptotic", "consistency", "large-sample"

    simulation-architect¶

    Monte Carlo study design.

    Expertise: - Simulation parameters - Performance metrics - Replication strategies - Results synthesis

    Activates on: "simulation", "Monte Carlo", "performance"

    algorithm-designer¶

    Statistical algorithm development.

    Expertise: - Computational efficiency - Numerical stability - Convergence criteria - Algorithm optimization

    Activates on: "algorithm", "implementation", "computational"

    numerical-methods¶

    Numerical optimization and computation.

    Expertise: - Root finding - Optimization algorithms - Numerical integration - Linear algebra

    Activates on: "optimization", "numerical", "computation"

    computational-inference¶

    Computational statistical inference.

    Expertise: - Bootstrap methods - MCMC - EM algorithm - Resampling methods

    Activates on: "bootstrap", "MCMC", "EM", "resampling"

    statistical-software-qa¶

    Software quality assurance.

    Expertise: - Unit testing - Validation studies - Numerical accuracy - Edge case handling

    Activates on: "testing", "validation", "quality assurance"

    Activates on: "journal", "publication", "submission"

    Activates on: "explain", "communicate", "presentation"
